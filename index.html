Stereo Speakers

Front-facing stereo speakers are simply awesome and superior to other speaker setups like mono or stereo side-firing speakers, combination of bottom-firing speaker and earpiece speaker, or rear-facing speaker.

16:9 Aspect Ratio

Why not the taller aspect ratio like 18:9 or 21:9? They are simply not very useful and what make the smartphone unnecessarily more expensive. 16:9 aspect ratio is good for watching videos and media contents without black bars on the sides or cropped content. For taller aspect ratio like 18:9 or 21:9, if the OLED screen tech is used, the black bars do not affect the battery usage, but for LCD, the backlight still has to be on for the black bar areas although no content is being shown.

Tactile Physical Keyboard

Tired of smudging your screen when typing? A physical keyboard comes to the rescue. More satisfying typing experience with all keys configurable for shortcuts. The brightness of keys' backlight is depending on lighting condition.

Importance of Bezels

With the bezeless trend getting all the love, having bezel as the place to hold your very expensive smartphone is very important especially in landscape mode while watching videos or playing games. This ensure that you do not accidentally touch the screen or drop your precious smartphone.
